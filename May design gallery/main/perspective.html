How can parents quickly tidy up the Toys?


Date : April 30th 2018

Kids : Liu: 5+ years old; Mia: 3 years old;


It’s not a rare case to have a mess house when you have kids. They can pull every out in 10 minutes and leave you a pile of stuffs to pack up after having fun with them. I think most of the parents feel quite headache about tiding up the toys.

What should we do? What this situation similar to? Just like finishing dinner, there are lots of dishes and cooking utensils waiting us to tidy up. if we don’t clean up right away after dinner, there will be a mess. However, there are lots of instruments to help us clean it up. We have dished washer, cabinet, dish hanger and so on. The cabinet and dish hanger help us divide the position of placing the utensils, plates, bowls, forks and chopsticks. Companies of kitchen ware try their best to make our work easier.

Kids have much more toys nowadays, comparing with my generation. Considering the toys, similar to the utensils, a container which is well designed for the toys would be popular.


Design inspiration:

I have an interesting idea that, the toy container can be an intelligent one. There are buttons or control panel to control which box you want to open it, this sound like a fiction movie. If the kids take something out, but didn’t put it back, there will be a signal to remind them, which might help the kids to develop a good habit.

The container has different rooms which have labels on it for different varieties of stuffs. The material of the container could be an important part of this product, it is ideal to be foldable and size changeable. Of course, it can be a normal one.

How to invoke interesting topic on Livestream (For teens)


Date : Feb 14th 2018

Dr. Danielle Lottridge


Part one

I went to Dr.Danielle’s lecture just now, she showed us the experiment of multitasking for student, and showed us and interesting phenomenon about teenager’s livestream and video chat. People are exposed to the world full of multitasks, people are able to do multitask at the same time.

Her experiment on Livestream of teenagers found that teens(13-17) usually do not know what to do and what to say while they are starting the camera. They might do what their audiences want them to do, most of them have no plan at the beginning. What she designed is to develop a small screen to show people her Instagram, Facebook photo or other thing else, try to invoke the topic; It’s quite interesting, isn’t it? First, this is a common phenomenon, people don’t know what to do, but they want to please their viewer. Second, frame the relevant incident into the Livestream is a creative design which we can apply to different design.


Design inspiration:

teens try to get rid of boredom on Livestream and Videochat. They have plenty hours to spend with themselves at night, at weekends, at home. They cannot find anything to do and they felt bored…

Part Two

Another interesting example is Youtube kids, It show the kids several videos at the same time, they can switch to other show quickly.


Design inspiration:

Kids and teenagers have no idea what to do sometimes, give them the choice, choose for them. When the kids don’t know what to do on weekend, show them the choice, design a web full of the activities that kids or teens can go. Sometimes parents have no idea where they can take their kids to, just show them the activities, club, classes they can join, does it sound interesting? When parents don’t know what to buy for their kids, it's good to let them know these toys are the top sale, those are the most popular toys, You can easily sell the product by providing some suggestion.

Why kids are obssessed with electronic game?


Date : Feb 4th 2018

Kids : Liu: 5+ years old; Mia: 3 years old;


Sometimes we would think kids are obsessed with electronic devices these day, but are we realize that the situation of our kids spending large amounts of time in computer game is are due to parents, who don’t have enough time and energy to play with them. In addition, they don’t have pals. Actually, they want to race, play game of hide and seek and other tricky game, interactive game with you, not an electronic one.

Girl is different from boy, Mia prefer to play babysitting game, cooking game…, she not only want a doll, but also want to place the doll in the cradle, hug it. Inspired from it, it will be amazing to design the whole set of toys, including the environment, devices, especially create a story for it.

Story is an eternal theme of kids, they enjoy stories from TV and books. They can also create stories by themselves while playing toys. However, kids cannot read before six years old, Story books full of words will not interest them if parents don’t read the stories for them. Audio books will be an alternative choice for them. Children’s song player and a story teller will be amazing for them.


They always asks me to play toys with them. You need to be creative and very patient and make them feel sense of achievement while playing with them. They would act as a restaurant owner, racing driver and other different charactor to simulate the adults' world.
